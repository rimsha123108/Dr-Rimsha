About Us

At Dr. Rimsha RPM Center, we provide personalized physiotherapy treatments to help you recover, regain strength, and live a healthier life.

Founder & CEO

Dr. Rimsha Sadiq
Dr. Rimsha Sadiq holds a degree in Physiotherapy and a Master’s in Neuromuscular Rehabilitation, complemented by a Postgraduate Diploma from FIFA Excellence Medical Centre, Dubai. She is a certified Acupuncture and Dry Needling Therapist, Kinesio Taping Specialist and Cupping Therapist.

Vision

To empower individuals to achieve a pain-free, healthier, and more active lifestyle by delivering world-class physiotherapy care. We aspire to become the most trusted physiotherapy and rehabilitation center in Pakistan, recognized for excellence, compassion, and innovation in patient care.

Mission

To design personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient’s individual needs, and to deliver exceptional physiotherapy care with compassion, dedication, and professional excellence.

Our Core Values

We believe in compassion, integrity, innovation, and teamwork to ensure the highest standards of patient care.
